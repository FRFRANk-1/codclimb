Features
Everything you need to plan a perfect day on the wall.
🎯

Climbability score

A single 0–100 score per crag, weighted by temperature, dryness, humidity, wind, and cloud cover. No more guessing.

🌧️

Rain & dryness tracker

See exactly how long ago it rained and get warnings when rain is approaching — so your limestone isn't a waterfall.

📅

Weekend trip planner

CodClimb ranks your saved crags by the best Saturday and Sunday forecast windows. Just show up and climb.

👥

Community reports

Climbers post real-time condition updates with photos. Seepage, rockfall, fixed gear status — straight from the crag.

🗺️

Score map

Browse all 123 crags on a map with color-coded score pins. Green means go, red means rest day.

🔔

Condition alerts

Set a score threshold for your home crag and get notified the moment conditions improve. No more checking every morning.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

CodClimb collects the minimum data needed to function:

  • Email address and display name (only if you create an account)
  • Profile photo (only if you upload one)
  • Condition reports and photos you voluntarily submit
  • Saved crags (stored locally on your device)
  • Approximate location (only when you use the map, never stored)

What we don't collect

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites
  • We do not show ads
  • Anonymous sessions require no account and store no personal data

Third-party services

CodClimb uses the following services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and cloud storage of condition reports
  • Open-Meteo — weather data (no account or personal data required)
  • Unsplash — crag photos (no personal data shared)

Data deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile tab in the app. Condition reports you've posted will also be removed.
