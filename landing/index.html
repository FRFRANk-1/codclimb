The Scoring Model
Built by climbers, for climbers.

The 0–100 score is built from five real weather factors, each weighted by how much it actually affects climbing. Not a black box — tap any factor in the app to see exactly what's driving your score.

Temperature
30%
Dryness
25%

20%
Wind
15%
Cloud cover
10%

Ideal temperature: 55–68°F. Dryness clock starts after the last rain and hits 100% at 24h. Wind sweet spot: 5–15 mph. Cloud cover adapts to your sky preference.

How it works
From forecast to send in three steps.
1

Find your crag

Search or browse the map. 123 US crags across every major climbing region, with filters for rock type, style, and sun exposure.

2

Check the score

See the current climbability score, live timestamp, factor breakdown with plain-English explanations, hourly forecast, and community reports.

3

Go climb

Pack the tape, drive to the crag, send your project. Post a condition report with photos to help the community.
